The Golden Hack 2019. Roomates and a four-man team

Business Lead: Sebastian Sypien
Design Lead: Darko Cejkov
Developer Lead: Owen Vanvulkenburg, Jordan Harris

This product was inspired by security and quality of life. Having an increasing demand of the cloud and the internet-of-things, we thought
of a solution to very confidential documents. Would you put your SSN or insurance details on a file somewhere in the cloud, without knowing how secure it is?
Our market research lead us to understand that not many people know exactly what their insurance covers, and by how much financially.
Despite spending thousands per year on insurance, we often forget how we're insured. This is why we prototyped Insure Data Safe.
A very safe way to store multiple types of insurance with attention to details like expiry, coverage amounts, and the nitty gritty policies.
Why file through cabinets to find papers when it's all here? With no outside access, your information is safe with us.
